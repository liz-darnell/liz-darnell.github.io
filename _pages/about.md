# About My Research 
I am in year 2 of my PhD and am researching trust of AI tools within the workplace. I am approaching this via mixed methods to work on 
1. Improving the reproducability of trust measurement methods,
2. increase understanding of how individuals trust these tools and how they conceptualise that trust, and
3. to eventually begin to research how explainability (XAI) impacts trust of AI for end-users. 

As part of my PhD I aim to have my contributions be: 
1. A protocol to measure and understand trust of AI tools that is context agnostic, and 
2. a clearer understanding of how end-users trust AI tools. 

## Current Work 
- Completing analaysis of first study on agentic IDEs
- Writing and preparing confirmation report for Congirmation Exam at end of year 2 (expected date: September 2026)

## Next Steps

  - RAG bot style tool for policy docs
